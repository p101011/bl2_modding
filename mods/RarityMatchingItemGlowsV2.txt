#<RarityMatchingItemGlows>

    #<Description>

        By Aaron0000

        Gives the glowy bits on non-elemental weapons/grenades a change of color to match their rarity.

        Note;

        E-Tech weapons normally would have their glow overwritten by elemental glows. There is an E-Tech specific option for overriding elemental glows in the E-Tech section.

        Maliwan weapons normally would have their glow overwritten by elemental glows, so don't expect to see rarity glows on them.

        Torgue weapons do not change color properly due to the innate explosive glow of the manufacturer. Disable the explosive glows in "Elemental Emissive Disabler" if you want Torgue guns to have rarity-matching glows.

        Gemstone glows override the elemental glows, there isn't a way around this.

        If you want to disable the elemental glows for all weapons, find the section at the bottom labeled "Elemental Emissive Disabler" and toggle it on with FilterTool (or manually remove the #'s in the code.)

        Examples;

        http://imgur.com/a/4TF9w

    #</Description>
