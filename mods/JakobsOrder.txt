#<Jakobs Order>

    #<Description>

        By Aaron0000

        Converts the Order into a Jakobs Roid shield.

        Features;

        No shield capacity, boosts health instead (and does not have damage resistance).

        Spawns as a Jakobs shield instead of Bandit.

        Existing Orders will remain Bandit.

        Can spawn with elemental capacitors, unlike the Rough Rider.

        NOTE;

        There is an option to give the Order a Jakobs skin similar to the Rough Rider. This will however affect all Rare Roid shields.

    #</Description>
